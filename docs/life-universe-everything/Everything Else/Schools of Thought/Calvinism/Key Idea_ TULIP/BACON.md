[Doctrines of
Grace](https://calebsnotes.brick.do/the-doctrines-of-grace-B1QRdP6lQJ1K)
(a.k.a.
[TULIP](https://calebsnotes.brick.do/key-idea-tulip-BYV5y5k3lPek)) using
plain language.
